Optional playground

Write NTL in the browser

This is a simple in-browser editor for writing snippets, saving drafts, and sharing small examples. It is not trying to replace a full IDE. It just gives people a place to play without leaving the site.

The editor remembers your draft in the browser and keeps the experience light. If you later wire this to a real runtime, the same page can grow with you.
