# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in the Lunex project a welcoming experience for everyone, regardless of age, background, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

**Positive behaviors include:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language.
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accepting constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users.
- Showing empathy toward other contributors.

**Unacceptable behaviors include:**

- Insulting, derogatory, or personal attacks.
- Public or private harassment of any form.
- Publishing someone's private information without consent.
- Trolling, deliberate disruption of discussions, or sustained off-topic posts.
- Any conduct that would be considered unprofessional in a collaborative setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, and any other channel where contributors interact on behalf of the Lunex project.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project maintainer via the contact listed on the [GitHub profile](https://github.com/Megamexlevi2). All reports will be reviewed promptly and kept confidential.

Maintainers reserve the right to remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who violate these standards.
